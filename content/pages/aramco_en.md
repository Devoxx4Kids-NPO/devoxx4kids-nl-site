## Aramco Europe Supports Devoxx4Kids: Reaching More Children with IT Education

Devoxx4Kids Netherlands has been committed for years to introducing children to technology in a playful and accessible way. During special Devoxx4Kids days, primary school students and children in the first two years of secondary education get the chance to experiment with programming, robotics, and digital safety — guided by enthusiastic volunteers from the IT sector.  

  
Until now, these events have taken place at external companies that generously offer their locations. But thanks to an annual donation from Aramco Europe, the foundation can significantly expand its reach and impact.  

### What This Contribution Enables

Aramco Europe’s support allows Devoxx4Kids to:  

- Purchase additional hardware, such as laptops and robots, enabling multiple workshops to run simultaneously.  
- Independently organize events in community centers or other accessible locations, ensuring that children outside the corporate world also gain access to IT education.  
  
This expansion means Devoxx4Kids can operate more flexibly and reach more children — especially in neighborhoods where access to technology is not a given.  

### About Aramco Europe

Aramco Europe is part of one of the world’s largest integrated energy and chemicals companies. From their offices across Europe, they contribute to reliable energy supply, technological innovation, and social progress. Aramco Europe strongly believes in the power of innovation and education to strengthen communities and prepare future generations for a rapidly changing world. By investing in STEM programs for young people — with a special focus on underserved groups — Aramco Europe actively supports talent development and the enhancement of digital skills across Europe.  

### Building Digital Opportunities Together

Thanks to Aramco Europe’s involvement, Devoxx4Kids is one step closer to its ambition of giving all children access to IT education. It’s a great example of how businesses and social initiatives can work together toward a future where technology is accessible to everyone.
